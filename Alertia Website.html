Combat mechanics, skill trees, and magic system reference.

Combat System

Guard

Guard

No knowledge of enemy attacks required. Player takes defensive stance — movement restricted, incoming damage reduced, may reduce negative effects. Viable throughout the entire game though effectiveness decreases at higher difficulties.

Basic BlockLv1 · 1pt · -20% damage
Reinforced GuardLv5 · 4pt · -30% + debuff resist
Counter StanceLv10 · 6pt · reflects 5% ATK
Master GuardLv15 · 8pt · stuns weak enemies
FortressLv20 · 12pt · HP regen while guarding
Dodge

Dodge

Requires understanding of enemy attacks. Real-time avoidance during enemy turn — inputs left, right, jump. Avoids all damage if successful. Full damage taken if failed.

Basic DodgeLv3 · 0pt · unlocks system
Swift ReflexesLv6 · 4pt · easier timing
Punisher RollLv12 · 6pt · 5–10% ATK on dodge
Expert EvasionLv18 · 8pt · partial hits reduced
Phantom StepLv22 · 12pt · evasion bonus after dodge
Parry

Parry

Requires precise timing and understanding of attacks. Strict timing window — success negates damage and deals counter damage. Failure increases damage taken 1.3–1.5x. High risk, high reward.

Basic ParryLv8 · 0pt · unlocks system
DeflectLv12 · 4pt · -20% follow-up damage
RiposteLv16 · 6pt · 25% ATK counter
Perfect TimingLv20 · 8pt · tighter window, more damage
Master ParryLv25 · 12pt · stun + 1.5x damage
Burst

Burst

Requires mastery of enemy timing. Consumes SP/MP. Functions like advanced parry with stricter timing. Success: massive damage, ends enemy turn, +7% accuracy. Failure: stunned, cannot parry, increased damage, cooldown triggered.

Burst StrikeLv18 · 12pt · unlocks Burst ability

Cannot be spammed — gated by level, cost, and cooldown.

Magic System

The five holy magics are the original forms, now extinct. Modern magic was refined from them after the Great Magic War. Dokuri gains each beast magic as he defeats their soul — his magic tree expands with the story. At the merge, all magic is spent on the sealing.

Water Magic

Holy magic of the Merfolk. Controls water, tides, fluid movement.

Refined into: Ice Magic

Unlocked: After defeating Naykala (Phase 2)

Wind Magic

Holy magic of the Oranata. Controls air currents, speed, force.

Refined into: Electric Magic

Unlocked: After defeating Hisanera (Phase 1)

Light Magic

Holy magic of the Fae. Controls radiance, illusion, revelation.

Refined into: Fire Magic

Unlocked: After defeating Oberon (Phase 2)

Poison Magic

Holy magic of the Naga. Controls toxins, corrosion, affliction.

Refined into: Plant Magic

Unlocked: After defeating Tesnata (Phase 1)

Steel Magic

Holy magic of humans. The first manufactured magic. Dokuri's base ability — enables temple entry.

Refined into: Stone Magic

Spent sealing Charybdis at the merge — Dokuri gains Stone Magic in its place

Progression Flow

Early Game

Guard and basic Dodge dominate. Player learns enemy patterns safely. Steel magic is Dokuri sole tool. First boss unlocks Poison magic from Tesnata.

Mid Game

Dodge becomes more refined. Parry unlock introduces skill expression. Wind magic added after Hisanera. Two magic trees running alongside combat skill tree.

Late Game / Phase 2

Parry mastery and optimization. Burst becomes practical. Light magic after Oberon, Water after Naykala. Four magic trees plus combat system — Dokuri carries all five holy magics simultaneously.

End Game

Players chain Dodge to Parry to Burst. Build optimization through bonus stats. All five magic trees available. At the merge, steel magic is spent sealing Charybdis — all other magic is lost with it. Dokuri gains Stone Magic, the modern refinement of steel, as what remains.
